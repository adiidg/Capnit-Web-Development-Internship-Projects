Introduction to Data Science

Data science is an interdisciplinary field that uses scientific methods, algorithms, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data. It is closely related to fields like machine learning, statistics, and artificial intelligence.

The data science process typically involves collecting data, cleaning and preparing the data, analyzing and modeling the data, and interpreting the results to make informed decisions. It has applications in various industries such as healthcare, finance, marketing, and more.

In this blog post, we will explore the foundational concepts of data science, its methodologies, and how it can be applied to solve real-world problems.
